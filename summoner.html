Summoner

April 2018

Unity, Blender, Substance Painter, Photoshop, Foam Core, & Makey-Makey


Summoner is a co-operative, two-player platforming game exploring the relationship between tangible and virtual interfaces in games. It was a solo-development project, created for a Project Studio class to experience the entire game development pipeline, from concept art, to level design, to 3D modelling, and programming. The game’s environment is inspired by ancient Chinese architecture and aims to showcase elements of Chinese history. Player One plays as a young girl exploring the world, looking for her family’s lost heirlooms. Player Two acts as her protector, summoning structures in the virtual world by touching the corresponding tangible figurines in the physical world.
