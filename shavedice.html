Strawberry Shaved Ice

September 2018

Blender & Photoshop


This project was created for Sketchfab's low-poly food modelling challenge. I wanted to experiment with hand-painting a diffuse-only texture and how a low-poly model can be brought to life with just digital painting. It was a fun challenge to stay within a low poly count while still effectively conveying the object's silhouette.
