Become Part of the Community


Learn to Cook Become a Cook

As A Member...

As a member, keep an eye out on the calendar, looking for sessions that interest you. Register for cooking sessions on the events page, check off which ingredient you are going to bring, and mark your calendars! During the session, meet new local people, learn to cook a specially prepared recipe and enjoy your creation with new friends. After the session, rate your cook on their page and look out for more events.



As A Cook...

As a cook, create unique recipes, teach the community your imaginative creations, and host cooking sessions. Don't worry about buying supplies, just list what you need on the events page and the members will bring it. Treat each session as an opportunity to experiment, meet new and interesting people, and eat great food!
