A passion for food, a passion to create.
We are a small community with a big heart.

What's the idea?

The idea is to bring together a community of students who want to learn how to cook from scratch, to test new and exciting recipes or to simply learn how to cook foods they've never been introduced to before. It's a way of creating a diverse audience of people with one common goal - to learn and develop skills.

How does it work? Easy! Take a look at our calendar to see where, what and when events are taking place. Once inside an event page, you'll see the required ingredients one can sign up to bring, or sign up for dish duty in return for the meal. You don't want to leave the host alone to clean up after 10 people, do you? Once you've reserved your ingredient or chore, you'll be signed up for that class!
