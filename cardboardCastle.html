Cardboard Castle Arcade

Relish Studios | Roblox | 2024




Cardboard Castle Arcade is an all-ages multiplayer Roblox experience with fun and competitive minigames! I worked with the team to establish the theme and design of the game, brainstorm game design ideas, and designed, created, and implemented the environments. I also designed, created, rigged, and animated the character assets for the mini-game Lunch Time Rush.



Environments

I designed the levels for the Ball Brawl and Lunchtime Rush minigames, beginning with a block out, then noting feedback and adjustments after playtesting with the team. Once the level design was complete, I modelled, textured, and implemented the finished environment assets using Blender and Roblox Studio.



Characters

The team decided to create the Lunchtime Rush minigame based on an idea I had for a “restaurant shooter,” where the player runs around trying to feed as many NPCs as possible. I designed the NPC characters, modelled, and textured them. I also rigged and created the chomping animation that plays when the player feeds them.
