Throne Room

January 2017

Blender & Photoshop


With this project, I started with a simple block-out in Blender of the basic design and layout of the room. Then, I took it into Photoshop and started to paint! I had the idea of an giant ancient whale's ribcage becoming the main feature of this environment.
