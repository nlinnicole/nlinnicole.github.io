Greek Tholos

August 2019

Blender, 3D Coat, Photoshop, & Unity


A personal project inspired by the tholos, a type of round temple found in ancient greek and roman architecture. I wanted to explore more in the world of handpainting, especially with foliage. I also wanted to push myself with the lighting by setting the environment at night. To play with an interactive version, click the link below!
