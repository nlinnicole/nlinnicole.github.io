Jerusalem Monastic Complex

November 2018

Photoshop


This project was created for an Art History of Jerusalem class. These designs are based off of historical accounts and descriptions found in academic papers about a monastic complex built by the Crusaders in 1099 on the Temple Mount in the city of Jerusalem. These illustrations and the accompanying essay are to be published in the Jerusalem Art History Journal in 2019.
