Jerusalem Monastic Complex

November 2018

Blender & Photoshop


This project was created for an Art History of Jerusalem class. These designs are based off of historical accounts and descriptions found in academic papers about a monastic complex built by the Crusaders in 1099 on the Temple Mount in the city of Jerusalem. Read the accompanying essay published in the Jerusalem Art History Journal Vol.5 here!
